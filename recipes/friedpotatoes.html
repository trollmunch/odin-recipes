Fried Potatoes

Description

This fried potatoes recipe is something my mom made all the time when I was growing up. It suits me, Cannon, perfectly as it doesn't contain any icky onions in it.

While this recipe is delicious, be warned, it is not healthy in the slightest.

Ingredients

  • Two Italian Sausage Links
  • 5 pound bag of potatoes
  • One Stick of Butter
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Optional: Cheese, Mayo, Ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat a Skillet to Medium Heat (5.5/10)
  2. Slice Potatoes into 1/4-1/2 inch slices - optional: peel potatoes first
  3. Slice Sausage into 1/2 inch slices
  4. Combine Potatoes and Sausage in a mixing bowl and drizzle lightly with olive oil
  5. Mix it based on feelings onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper
  6. Place butter on bottom of preheated skillet and allow to melt
  7. Dump potato and sausage mix into skillet and cover
  8. Stir/Flip every 10 minutes to prevent burning
  9. In about 45-50 minutes you will have a perfect dish of Mom's Fried Potatoes
  10. Optional: Add toppings such as cheese, mayo, or ketchup to enhance flavor!
