Crepes

Description

All other recipes on this website have come from mom, however, this one comes from her sister.

Crepes were always something to look forward to. We would have them each year at Christmas. Hope you enjoy them as much as I do.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 Cup Flour
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 6 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 tubs of Cool Whip
  • 16 oz Cream Cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup Powdered Sugar
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • Butter
  • Optional: Strawberries and Blueberries

Crepe Steps

  1. Combine flour, salt, and eggs; beat until smooth
  2. Gradually add milk; mixing until well blended
  3. Heat skillet to Medium-Low Heat (4/10)
  4. Pour 1/4 cup batter into lightly buttered skillet
  5. After about 2 minutes, flip the crepe and continue to cook for 1 additional minute

Filling Steps

  1. Blend softened cream cheese with powdered sugar and vanilla extract
  2. Gently mix in Cool Whip

Serving

  1. Fill finished crepes with filling mix and fold into the shape of an enchillada
  2. Top with berries of your choice
  3. Enjoy!
