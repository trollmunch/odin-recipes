Deer Chili

Description

This is the most basic chili recipe that is perfect for a picky eater who hates tomatoes!

Derrived from Mom's Chili Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Pounds of Venision
  • 2 Large Cans of Chili Beans
  • One packet of chili seasoning
  • One Bottle of Tomato Juice
  • Optional: Sour Cream, Shredded Cheese, Fritos

Steps

  1. Prepare a crockpot on low heat
  2. Brown deer meat in skillet
  3. Once deer meat is browned, add in chili seasoning. You may also need to add some water depending on the fat content of the meat.
  4. Combine meat, two cans of beans, and tomato juice in crockpot.
  5. Wait approximatly 2-8 hours, depending on how flavorful you want it
  6. Serve with sour cream and shredded cheese over top Fritos
